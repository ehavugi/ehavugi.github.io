--- layout: page title: About Me --- Emmanuel Havugimana is a Graduate Research Associate (Ph.D.) at Arizona State University. He completed his B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Master of Engineering in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. His current research interests are in high frequency power magnetics and power electronics. Before ASU, he worked as an Electrical Engineer at an electric mobility startup, Ampersand Ltd, in Kigali, Rwanda
